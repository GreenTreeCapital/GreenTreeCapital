About Green Tree Capital

Green Tree Capital is a personal investment journal run by two individual investors who share an interest in sustainable, long-term capital allocation. We are not an investment firm and we do not manage money on behalf of others.

Our Focus

  • Long-term equity investing with an emphasis on quality and resilience
  • Medium-term tactical positioning around macro and valuation shifts
  • Select equity and forex views, always sized and risk-managed carefully
  • Clear written documentation of our thought process and performance

How to Use This Site

The site is designed for learners who want to understand how a real portfolio is constructed and adjusted over time. You can:

  • Read Market News to see how we interpret major events
  • Review Fund Performance charts to track our own progress
  • Join the Discord to see position updates and discuss ideas with others

What you will not find is advice telling you what to do. Your capital, your risk tolerance and your time horizon are unique to you.

We are not regulated financial advisors or portfolio managers. All content is based on our personal opinions and experience and is shared strictly for educational and informational purposes.
